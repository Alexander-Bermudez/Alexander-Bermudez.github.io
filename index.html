About Me

Hi! I’m Alex, a passionate and experienced web developer based out of Rancho Cucamonga, CA with a degree in Computer Science from California State University, San Marcos. Over the past 5 years, I’ve honed my expertise in front-end development, creating intuitive, visually engaging, and responsive user interfaces.

My journey began as a Student Web Developer at Cal State San Marcos, where I gained foundational experience learning how to manage a website, communicate with clients, and about accessibility(WCAG). For the last 3 years, I’ve been shaping digital experiences as a UI Web Developer at California Baptist University in Riverside, CA.

In my current role, I lead the design, development, and maintenance of user interfaces for both marketing portfolios and internal web applications, always prioritizing user needs and seamless functionality. My advanced skills in HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, and XSLT empower me to bring concepts to life, delivering polished and efficient web solutions. I'm passionate about creating impactful web experiences that are not only functional but also beautiful and user-centered.

During my time at California Baptist University, I also took a large role in a complete website redesign, reinvigorating the campus website's user interface. This redesign spanned many months and involved a user-interface rework with new animations and modules for ease of use, a site-restructure, and an analytics conversion into GA4.

In my free time, I enjoy watching baseball and football, playing video games, going to theme parks, and building custom PCs as well.
